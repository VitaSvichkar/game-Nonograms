General Rules of Nonogram:

  • The numbers indicate groups of consecutive filled cells in that row or column. For example, a clue of "4 1" means there is a block of 4 consecutive filled cells followed by at least one empty cell and then a block of 1 filled cell.
  • Your task is to correctly color the cells to reveal a hidden picture, all while adhering to the number clues for each row and column.
  • A cross (usually marked with the right mouse button) means that the cell must remain empty.
  • The puzzle is solved when all the cells are correctly filled or marked, and the image appears.
